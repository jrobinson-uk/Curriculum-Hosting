As our suite of learning products grows to serve different audiences and contexts, consistency is essential to ensure they are all working towards the same goal and enabling young people to realise their full potential through the power of computing and digital technologies. A consistent approach prevents us from presenting ideas in conflicting or confusing ways, creating a more unified experience for our users.

To ensure consistency over time and across teams, we are committed to documenting the design decisions we make. Without documented design decisions, our reasoning, rationale and our mistakes and learning from them become institutional knowledge that can easily be lost. This makes it difficult to replicate successful designs or onboard new team members. 

By ensuring we record our rationale, we make our design process more transparent and repeatable, which is crucial for maintaining consistency and quality as we grow.

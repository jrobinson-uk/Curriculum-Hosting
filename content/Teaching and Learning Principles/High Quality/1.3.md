Together, our range of learning experiences are designed for lasting impact, not just one-off engagement. We help learners build practical skills and deeper understanding, anticipating and addressing misconceptions along the way. Our learning designers ensure that every learning experience includes opportunities for learners to demonstrate their understanding, and we also design our explanations and activities carefully to avoid introducing new misconceptions

Where appropriate, we integrate meaningful assessments that are directly linked to learning objectives. This allows learning to be demonstrated, measured, and reinforced, and it provides learners with feedback that helps build confidence and allows them to progress.

We recognise that to have the greatest impact, our content must be adaptable. No single resource can perfectly meet the needs of every learner, educator, and context. 

Adaptability is about ensuring our content has the right properties to be ready for adaptation. Some aspects of adaptability are supported by other themes and principles already discussed, the level of English for example is primarily about making our content more accessible, but it also makes it more adaptable. This section focuses on principles that specifically address adaptability

Adaptability not only empowers our community, it also streamlines our own work, and is fundamental to achieving our global mission.

The taxonomy  framework was developed by the Raspberry Pi Foundation to categorise computing conceptual knowledge.

The framework demonstrates the breadth of computing as a discipline and provides language to describe the different areas of study and competencies.

Each area or “strand” provides examples of concepts and skills that learners would ideally develop at each stage of their formal computing education

All formal education content produced by the Raspberry Pi Foundation maps to this framework.

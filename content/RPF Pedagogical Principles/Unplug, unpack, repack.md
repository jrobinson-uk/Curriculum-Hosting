## Unplug, unpack, repack

Teach new concepts by first unpacking complex terms and ideas, exploring these ideas in unplugged and familiar contexts, then repacking this new understanding into the original
concept. This approach ([semantic waves](QR06.md)) can help pupils develop a secure understanding of complex concepts.

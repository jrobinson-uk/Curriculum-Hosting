> [!educator] Educators

> In machine learning ML , training data refers to the examples in the form of data used to train ML models. ML developers build models to work out patterns in the training data, which can be used to generate predictions about new data. For example, an ML developer builds a speech recognition application. The training data might include lots of examples of people speaking, in different accents or tones of voice. The more the training data represents reality, the better the model is likely to perform.

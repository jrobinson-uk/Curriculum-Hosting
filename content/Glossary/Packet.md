> **Aliases :** payload, data packet

---

> [!primary] Younger Learners
> 
> Small chunks of a whole file that are sent (transmitted) over a network. Each packet contains a header and a payload

> [!secondary] Older Learners
> 
>  a segment of data, usually part of a larger message
> The name of the data structure at the internet layer. It is used to move the transport layer data from the source to the destination across a network.

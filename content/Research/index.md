## About us

The Research team at the Raspberry Pi Foundation works in close partnership with the [Raspberry Pi Computing Education Research Centre](https://computingeducationresearch.org/) at the University of Cambridge.

Together, we combine research expertise with practical insight from the Foundation’s educational work to better understand how computing is taught and learned. Our collaboration helps ensure that research findings directly inform our programmes, resources, and ways of working.

A key focus of our work is supporting young people who are under-represented in computing or who experience educational disadvantage, and translating research into practice to create long-term impact.
